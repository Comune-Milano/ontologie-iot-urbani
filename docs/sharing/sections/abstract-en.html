Abstract

The Sharing Mobility Module of the ontology suite for the Urban IoT Devices ([http://ww.w3id.org/urban-iot/sharing]()). This module reuses the concepts and the principal properties defined in the Core Module ([http://ww.w3id.org/urban-iot/core]()), in the Sharing Mobility domain. In particular, the module designs the aspects related to a service provider for the Sharing Mobility who manages the IoT vehicles in the urban area, the service usage by the users and the modelling of an IoT device which collects and sends data.
