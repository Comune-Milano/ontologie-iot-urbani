Abstract

The Core Module of the ontology suite for the Urban IoT Devices ([http://ww.w3id.org/urban-iot/core]()). This module defines the concepts and the principal properties that will be reused in other modules. In particular, the module designs the aspects related to a service provider who manages the IoT devices in the urban area, the service usage by the users and the modelling of an IoT device which collects and sends data.
